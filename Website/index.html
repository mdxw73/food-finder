Pantry View



Welcome

Pantry View offers users a revolutionary new way to cook for themselves. Instead of planning what to eat before a grocery trip and worrying whether you have enough at home, we give you the ability to instantly get recipes that you already have all the ingredients for. Use the built-in ingredient detector to identify ingredients from a photo of your fridge/pantry. Then instantly get recipes for those ingredients for everything from desserts to cocktails.

Features

Ingredient detector to identify ingredients in an image
Recipe search by ingredients
Recipe search by meal name, style, or genre
Recipe images, cooking time, ingredients, description, and instructions
Favorites to save the recipes you love
